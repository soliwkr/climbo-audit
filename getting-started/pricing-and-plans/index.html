    Pricing and Plans¶

    One-line summary: You pay €99/month to Climbo; you charge clients whatever you want — typical range is $99–$500/month.

    tags: [getting-started, pricing, business-model] sources: [BRocA7husPU, PQiNrTKVLOU, PLAYBOOK] last_updated: 2026-07-11

    Overview¶

    Climbo's pricing to you is simple: €99/month flat. Unlimited clients. All features. No per-seat fees, no percentage of revenue.

    Your pricing to clients is entirely up to you. You set the plans, the names, the prices, and what features each plan includes. Climbo's Plans section is where you create these.

    How it works¶

    What you pay Climbo¶

    • €99/month agency fee (billed to your card)
    • Plus Balance top-ups for SMS/WhatsApp/AI token usage (see [[features/balance-explained]])
    • No setup fees. No per-client fees.

    What you charge clients¶

    Create plans in your Climbo dashboard under Plans. Common structures in the community:

    Single-tier (simple start): - One plan at $99–$149/month - Includes: review collection, SEO Agent, GBP management - Easiest to sell, easiest to deliver

    Three-tier (scales revenue per client): | Tier | Price | What's included | |------|-------|----------------| | Starter | $99/mo | Review collection + SEO Agent | | Growth | $249/mo | + Social Agent + GBP posts | | Authority | $499/mo | + GEO Agent + AI search optimization |

    Community member wins at various price points: - "Picked up a new flooring contractor — $299/month" (Skool, 19 comments) - "1 x Beauty salon $1,800 annual" (Skool) - "5 restaurants on annual plan — 3,000€ in one week" (Skool, 28 comments)

    The math to $3k/month¶

    From Giacomo's training: - 30 face-to-face outreach per month - ~30% say yes to free trial (10 free trials/month) - ~30% of trials convert to paid ($100/month) - = 3 new paying clients per month - After 12 months: 36 paying clients × $100 = $3,600 MRR

    These are conservative numbers. Multiple community members have surpassed this.

    Free trials¶

    Climbo has a built-in free trial mechanism. You can give a client free access for 7–14 days (configurable). During the trial, they collect real reviews and see the system work. Once they see reviews coming in, conversion is almost guaranteed.

    Use coupons (Settings → Finance → Coupons) to: - Offer 100% discount for a defined period (free trial) - Give a fixed discount for early adopters - Create time-limited promotions

    Annual plans¶

    Offer annual pricing at a discount (e.g., 2 months free = 10× monthly price). Benefits: - You get cash upfront - Client is committed for 12 months - Zero churn risk for a year - Multiple community members have sold annual plans successfully

    Stripe integration¶

    Plans you create in Climbo connect directly to Stripe for automated billing. Clients enter their card once; billing is automatic forever. You do not chase invoices. See [[features/stripe-billing]] for setup details.

    Key details / Tips¶

    • Start simple. One plan at $100–$149/month is easier to close than explaining three tiers to a first prospect.
    • Annual upfront is your best retention tool. A client who paid for a year never churns in month 3.
    • Balance is separate from your plan fee. If you run SMS campaigns, make sure you have enough balance. Email is free.
    • Don't undercharge. Community feedback is clear: charging $99/month gets the same resistance as $149/month, but $149 gives you double the cushion.
