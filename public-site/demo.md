## Contenuto testuale

Climbo gives you a fully rebrandable platform that helps local businesses get more reviews, rank higher on Google, and get discovered on AI platforms. You sell it as your own software and build monthly recurring revenue.

Your white-label platform is fully translated. Deploy it in any market.

Your agency's job is to rank clients. The Review Marketing Flywheel™ is the system that does it. It starts with collecting reviews — the most trusted signal on Google and AI platforms. Every review gets an AI-written reply packed with local keywords, boosting Maps rankings. Every review becomes a social post, building trust and engagement. Every review feeds a website and blog that makes your client appear when someone asks ChatGPT for the best business in their city. One setup. Three AI agents. Ranking results your clients can see — and pay you monthly for.

Reviews are the #1 ranking signal on Google Maps and the primary trust signal on AI platforms like ChatGPT. The more reviews a business has — and the faster they accumulate — the higher it ranks. You set up the collection system once: QR codes at the counter, automated SMS and WhatsApp messages after each visit, and the Employee Reward System that turns staff into a review-collection machine. From that moment, the flywheel starts spinning automatically.

Every review gets an AI-written reply packed with the business name, service type, and city — the exact signals Google uses to rank businesses on Maps. Each reply is a micro-SEO action: it reinforces relevance, builds keyword density, and tells Google this business is active and trusted. Your client climbs the Local Pack rankings without writing a single word.

Every review becomes a branded social post — automatically published on Instagram, Facebook, and Google Business Profile with an AI-written caption. Consistent social activity is a ranking signal: it tells Google the business is active, builds trust with potential customers, and keeps the brand visible between visits. Your client stays top-of-mind without touching their phone.

When someone asks ChatGPT or Perplexity for the best dentist or top-rated plumber nearby, the GEO Agent makes sure your client is the answer. It builds and continuously updates a website and blog using the business's reviews and location data — structured specifically to rank on Google and to be cited by AI search tools.

Every local business client gets access to a fully branded AI assistant — under your brand's name and identity. Through a simple chat interface, they can send review request campaigns, update their Google Business Profile, add new contacts, write response templates, manage team members, and much more. No training required, no complex dashboards. Just a conversation that gets things done.

The Review Marketing Flywheel™ builds ranking authority across every platform where local customers search. Google Maps rankings improve as keyword-rich review replies accumulate. Google Search rankings improve as the GEO Agent publishes optimized content. AI platforms like ChatGPT and Perplexity start citing your client when someone asks for the best business in their area. All of this happens automatically — every day, for every client.

Manage every ranking client from a single agency dashboard. Monitor review velocity, ranking progress, and MRR. Set plans, white-label the platform, and track performance across all clients — all under your brand.

Turn every customer touchpoint into a 5-star review — automatically. From collection to response, every step is handled.

Full white-label customization — your logo, colors, domain, and AI agent names. Your clients see your SaaS, not ours.
