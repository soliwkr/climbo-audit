## Overview

Climbo is a SaaS platform built for agency owners, not for local businesses directly. You pay €99/month flat. You rebrand the entire platform with your name, logo, and domain. You resell it to local businesses (restaurants, dentists, gyms, law firms, contractors) at whatever price you choose — typically $99–$500/month per client. Every client you add increases your profit; your cost stays fixed at €99.

The core promise to your clients: more Google reviews, higher ranking, more customers — all automated. They do not log in daily. They do not manage anything. The system runs in the background.

## How it works

Climbo runs what the founder Giacomo calls the **Review Marketing Flywheel**:

```
Customer leaves a review
  → SEO Agent replies with keyword-rich text (Google Maps ranking improves)
  → Social Agent turns the review into a social post (GBP stays active)
  → GEO Agent publishes blog/web content (client appears on ChatGPT, Perplexity)
  → More visibility → more customers → more reviews
```

This cycle runs automatically once you complete the initial setup. Setup takes roughly 1–2 hours per new client. After that, your time commitment per client per month is close to zero.

**Three AI agents power the flywheel:**
- **SEO Agent** — automatically replies to Google reviews using the client's business name, city, and service keywords
- **Social Agent** — turns the best reviews into image + caption posts on Google Business Profile (GBP), Facebook, and Instagram
- **GEO Agent** — builds and updates a website/blog so the client gets cited by AI search engines like ChatGPT and Perplexity (released April 17, 2026)

## Key details / Tips

- **GBP** = Google Business Profile. It's the listing that appears when someone searches "dentist near me" on Google. This is the single most important asset for local businesses and the main thing Climbo manages.
- **Zero-gating:** Climbo does NOT secretly redirect unhappy customers away from Google. Every reviewer can always leave a public review. This is compliant with Google's April 2026 policy update.
- **21 review platforms supported:** Google is the priority, but Climbo also connects to Tripadvisor, Yelp, Booking.com, and 17 others.
- **White-label means invisible:** Your client sees your brand name, your domain, your AI assistant's name. Climbo is the engine; you are the face.
- **Break-even is one client.** If you charge €100/month, you profit from client 2 onward.
