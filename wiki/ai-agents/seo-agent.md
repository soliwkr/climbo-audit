## Overview

When a customer leaves a review on Google, the SEO Agent writes and publishes an AI reply within minutes. The reply includes:
- The client's business name
- The city or neighborhood
- The specific service mentioned in the review
- A natural, human-sounding tone

This matters for ranking because Google's algorithm scores how actively a business responds to reviews. A business with 100 reviews and 100 replies ranks better than one with 100 reviews and 0 replies — all else equal.

A typical AI reply might look like:

> "Thank you so much for your kind words, Maria! At [Business Name] in [City], we're passionate about [Service]. We're thrilled you had such a great experience and look forward to seeing you again!"

Simple. Relevant. Keyword-rich. Published instantly.

## How it works

### Setup (one time per client)

1. Go to the client's account in Climbo
2. Navigate to **AI Agents → SEO Agent**
3. Enable the agent
4. Configure:
   - **Keywords** — list the most important ones (business name, city, services)
   - **Reply language** — set to match the client's customers
   - **Reply length** — short (1–2 sentences), medium (3–4 sentences), or long
   - **Tone** — professional, friendly, formal, or custom prompt
   - **Additional instructions** — any specific phrases to include or avoid

5. Optionally: click "Reply to all past reviews" to retroactively reply to existing unanswered reviews. This one action can have an immediate positive effect on Maps ranking.

### Ongoing operation

Once enabled, the agent fires automatically every time a new review arrives. No action needed from you or the client.

The client can see all agent-generated replies in the Reviews section of their dashboard, and can edit or override any reply manually if they prefer a different tone for a specific review.

## Key details / Tips

- **Replying to negative reviews is just as important as positive ones.** A graceful, professional reply to a 1-star review shows potential customers that the business cares. The SEO Agent handles these too, but you may want to review negative replies before they go live. Check the agent settings for a "require approval for negative reviews" option.
- **Language matters.** If your client serves a French-speaking market, set the language to French. The reply will match the reviewer's expectation.
- **Custom prompt = full control.** You can write a custom prompt to match the exact voice of the business. Example: "Reply as a warm, family-run business. Always mention that we've been serving [City] since 2010."
- **Keywords are not spam.** The SEO Agent weaves keywords naturally. It does not keyword-stuff. Google can detect unnatural keyword insertion and it hurts rather than helps.
- The agent uses AI tokens from your Balance. For a client getting 30 reviews/month, token cost is a few cents.

## Common question: Is this compliant?

Yes. Replying to reviews is explicitly encouraged by Google. There is no policy against AI-assisted replies as long as the replies are genuine and relevant — which the SEO Agent is designed to produce.

The agent does NOT offer incentives in exchange for reviews, does NOT post reviews, and does NOT manipulate review content. It only responds.
