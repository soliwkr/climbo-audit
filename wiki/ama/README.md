## Overview

Climbo runs a weekly live AMA (Ask Me Anything) on YouTube hosted by Giacomo Chinellato (co-founder). Each AMA serves as a live product changelog — new features are demoed live, and community questions are answered. 25 of the 31 videos are AMA sessions; the rest are standalone training or promo content.

Note: `wS_Vtz4Pvkc` (Dec 2024, "Live streaming di Climbo") has no transcript available.
