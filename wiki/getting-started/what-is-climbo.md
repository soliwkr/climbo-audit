## Overview

Reputation management is not just getting Google reviews. Here is what it actually covers:

1. **Collect reviews** — get more positive reviews flowing consistently
2. **Protect the rating** — give unhappy customers a private feedback channel so they can be heard before posting publicly
3. **Reply to every review** — with AI-generated, keyword-rich responses
4. **Market with reviews** — share five-star reviews on social media and your website
5. **Keep listings accurate** — correct hours, photos, contact info on Google
6. **Get found on AI search** — make sure ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI answers mention the business

Climbo automates all six of these. You set it up once. The system runs from there.

## Why local businesses need this

The data is clear:

- 98% of customers read reviews before calling a local business (BrightLocal)
- Increasing a rating by just 0.5 stars leads to 59% more revenue (Harvard Business Review)
- Getting into the Google Maps top-3 results generates 126% more traffic (BrightLocal)
- Five-star profiles get 35% more calls than four-star profiles (Search Engine Land)

A dentist with 24 reviews while the competitor two blocks away has 350 reviews is losing patients every day. That dentist is your target client. They understand the problem immediately when you show them the comparison.

## What Climbo is not

- It is not a service you deliver manually. You set up automation; the software does the work.
- It is not visible to your clients as "Climbo." They see your brand name and logo.
- It is not a lead generation tool. It keeps businesses found once they have customers. Your job is to bring in the business owner as a client; their job is to serve their own customers.
- It is not a quick-rich scheme. Giacomo says this explicitly: it is a real business that requires consistent sales effort.

## The agency model in plain terms

You pay Climbo: **€99/month** (unlimited clients, all features).

You charge your clients: anywhere from $100 to $500+ per month depending on the package.

Your clients never know Climbo exists. They log into your branded platform at your custom domain. They see your AI assistant name. Every email they receive comes from your domain.

You are the agency. Climbo is your back-end.

## Key details / Tips

- Start by targeting businesses with **low review counts, stale ratings, and active competitors beating them on Google** — they feel the pain immediately
- The free trial strategy ("let me get you 5 reviews for free, no credit card") consistently converts — once a business owner sees reviews coming in, the sale is done
- Clients stay because the system keeps working. Churn comes from bad onboarding, not from the product failing.
