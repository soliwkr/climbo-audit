> **One-line summary:** The client portal is what your clients log into — a fully branded dashboard showing their review stats, rankings, agent activity, and GBP performance.

## Overview

When your client logs into `app.youragency.com` (your branded domain), they land on the client portal. This is their view — not your agency view.

The portal shows your client:
- How many reviews they have and how the count is growing
- Their Google rating trend
- What the AI agents have been doing
- How their GBP is performing (impressions, clicks, calls)
- Where they rank versus competitors

Clients do not see billing settings, other clients' data, or any reference to Climbo.

## How it works

### Home screen (updated July 9, 2026 — Home Charts)

The home screen is divided into two sections after the July 2026 update:

**Agent Activity (right side):**
- Live log of what each agent has done
- "SEO Agent replied to 3 reviews today"
- "Social Agent published 2 posts to GBP and Instagram"
- "GEO Agent updated the website with 1 new article"

**Growth Charts (left side):**
Three charts that update monthly, tracked from the day the client joined your platform:

1. **Reviews chart** — total review count over time (blue line) + average rating (orange line)
2. **Rankings chart** — Google Maps position for key search terms, compared to competitors
3. **GBP Performance chart** — impressions, clicks, direction requests, call clicks

Giacomo designed these charts specifically for retention: "Your clients buy results, not features. These charts show them that since they joined your platform, the most important KPIs are improving month over month."

### Other portal sections

**Reviews:**
- Inbox of all reviews across connected platforms
- Private feedback submissions from unhappy customers
- Ability to reply manually (or see AI replies already sent)

**Contacts:**
- Add customer contacts manually
- Upload CSV for bulk import
- View auto-send status for each contact

**Requests:**
- Send quick review request
- View request history and open/click rates

**Google Business Profile:**
- Edit business info (syncs to Google)
- Upload photos
- Publish posts and offers

**AI Agents:**
- View or adjust agent settings
- See agent activity log

**Settings:**
- Connect integrations (Google, Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp)
- Manage review templates

### AI Chatbot alternative

If you enable the AI Chatbot in your Brand settings, clients see a chat window instead of (or alongside) the full dashboard. They can ask their AI assistant to:
- "Send a review request to my last customer"
- "Update my business hours to 9am–7pm"
- "Add a new contact: John Smith, phone 555-1234"

This is particularly useful for clients who find dashboards overwhelming. The chatbot interface removes the learning curve entirely.

## What good portal design looks like for retention

From the community: clients stay when they can clearly see that the number is going up. The "before" screenshot you take on onboarding day becomes the evidence you show at day 90:
- "You had 12 reviews. You now have 47."
- "Your Google Maps ranking moved from position 8 to position 2 for 'dentist in [city].'"
- "Your GBP got 430 more impressions this month than last month."

These are the moments that lock in a long-term relationship.

## Key details / Tips

- **Set the baseline on day 1.** Screenshot the starting stats before anything runs. You need a before picture to show the after.
- **Teach the client where to look.** During onboarding, walk them through the dashboard. Show them the home charts. Tell them "this number will go up every week."
- **The gamification element.** Climbo's review stats show "you need X more five-star reviews to reach 4.8 stars" — clients compete against themselves to hit the next milestone.
- **Sentiment analysis.** Monthly reports analyze the text of all reviews to identify what customers love and what needs improvement. This is genuinely useful for the business, not just for marketing.
