## Overview

Climbo released its iOS and Android apps in April 2026. Both the agency view and the client view are available on mobile.

For clients, this is especially important: they can see their review count growing in real time on their phone. A notification arriving that says "You got a new 5-star review" — without them doing anything — is one of the strongest retention signals in the product.

## How it works

### For clients (local business owners)

The mobile app gives clients:
- **Review notifications** — push notification every time a new review arrives
- **Stats at a glance** — current rating, total reviews, recent activity
- **Quick requests** — send a review request to a customer directly from the phone
- **AI agent activity** — see that the SEO Agent has replied and the Social Agent has posted

Most clients will use the app only for the notification and the occasional stats check. They are not managing anything; they are watching the number go up.

### For agency owners

From the app you can:
- Monitor all clients' activity
- See incoming reviews across your client base
- Check MRR trends
- Respond to client questions

The mobile experience is lighter than the full desktop dashboard but covers the essentials for quick checks while away from your computer.

### Installation

- iOS: available on the App Store (search "Climbo")
- Android: available on Google Play Store
- Clients access the app using their credentials for your branded platform

> Note: The app may show the Climbo name during installation (App Store listing) even though the platform inside is white-labeled. This is a current limitation — clients who search the App Store will see "Climbo." Some agencies address this by directing clients to the mobile web version of their custom domain instead.

## Key details / Tips

- **Push notifications are a retention tool.** A restaurant owner who gets a notification at 9pm saying "3 new reviews today!" on their phone is a client who stays. Make sure clients install the app during onboarding.
- **Mobile web works too.** If a client doesn't want to install an app, your custom domain at `app.youragency.com` works well on mobile browsers.
- **Agency view on mobile.** Useful for checking in on clients while traveling, but full configuration and setup is easier on desktop.
- Announced and released: April 2026 (iOS and Android simultaneously).
